Amit is a software tester at RSA, and has been there for the past five years. As part of his job, he enjoys coding, dealing with application security and finding new ways to work with the team. He also helps as a co-editor for a quarterly testing magazine (in Hebrew) and gets a first peek at many great ideas. 
When not doing anything testing related or riding his bicycle, he enjoys playing some board games with friends and is a member is the Israeli roleplaying society.
